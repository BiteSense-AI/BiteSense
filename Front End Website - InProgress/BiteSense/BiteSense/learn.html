What types of bug bites can BiteSense identify?

BiteSense can accurately identify a variety of bug bites, including those from mosquitoes, fleas, bedbugs, ticks, and ants, using our advanced AI algorithms.

How does BiteSense provide treatment advice?

After identifying the type of bug bite, BiteSense offers personalized treatment suggestions based on best practices and recommendations from medical professionals.

Is my data safe with BiteSense?

Yes! We prioritize your privacy and security. Any data you share with us is protected and used solely to enhance your experience with the app.

Can I contribute to the bug bite heat maps?

Absolutely! We encourage users to report their experiences and sightings. Your contributions help create a comprehensive picture of insect activity in different areas.

What platforms does BiteSense support?

BiteSense is available on both iOS, Android, and the web, ensuring everyone can benefit from our innovative bug bite identification technology.

What should I do if I experience a severe reaction to a bug bite?

If you experience severe symptoms such as swelling, difficulty breathing, or excessive pain, please seek medical attention immediately. Our app can help identify the bite, but we always recommend consulting a healthcare professional for serious reactions.
